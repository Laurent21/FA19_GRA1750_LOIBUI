Sandra Bullock

Sandra Annette Bullock was born in Arlington, a Virginia suburb of Washington, D.C.

She now ranks as one of the most popular actresses in Hollywood. For her role in The Blind Side (2009) she won the Oscar, and her blockbusters The Proposal (2009), The Heat (2013) and Gravity (2013) made her a bankable star.

George Clooney
George Timothy Clooney was born on May 6, 1961, in Kentucky. He has Irish, English, and German ancestry.

Clooney received critical acclaim for his performance in "Up in the Air" and was nominated for several awards, including a Golden Globe and Academy Award.
